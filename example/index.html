PM5 Monitor

This application connects to a Concept2 PM5 monitor — over Bluetooth (wireless) or USB — and displays all workout variables it reports.

Requirements

  • Bluetooth: a Web Bluetooth browser (Chrome/Edge on desktop) and the PM5's wireless turned on.
  • USB: Chrome or Edge (Web HID is not supported in Firefox or Safari) with the PM5 connected by USB.
  • A PM5 attached to a Concept2 ergometer.

Usage

  • Pick Bluetooth or USB from the dropdown.
  • For Bluetooth, on the PM5: More Options → Turn Wireless ON. For USB, connect the PM5 to your computer.
  • Click Connect and pick the PM5 from the browser's device picker.
  • Click any field to highlight it.
  • Set up a workout on the PM5. Start rowing, skiing, or biking. Watch the numbers change.

Tested on Chrome on macOS. Source on GitHub.
